二、AI文创平台概述（小字介绍+展示图片）
二、 Overview of the AI Cultural Creation Platform

在数字化时代，AI技术为文创领域带来了前所未有的创作活力与创新可能，大量独特的AI文创作品不断涌现。然而，当前AI文创产业面临着严峻的版权归属权界定不清以及文创盗用乱象问题。我们的平台利用区块链去中心化、不可篡改、可追溯的特性，为AI文创作品提供权威、高效的确权服务。通过区块链技术，每一件AI文创作品从创作完成的那一刻起，其创作时间、创作者信息、作品内容等关键数据就被永久记录在区块链上，形成不可篡改的数字指纹，明确作品的版权归属，为创作者提供坚实的版权保护。

In the digital age, AI technology has brought unprecedented creative vitality and innovation possibilities to the cultural and creative fields, with a surge of unique AI cultural and creative works continuously emerging. However, the current AI cultural and creative industry faces severe challenges in clarifying copyright ownership and issues related to cultural and creative plagiarism. Our platform utilizes the decentralized, tamper-proof, and traceable characteristics of blockchain technology to provide authoritative and efficient rights confirmation services for AI cultural and creative works. Through blockchain technology, key data such as the creation time, creator information, and content of each AI cultural and creative work are permanently recorded on the blockchain from the moment of creation, forming an unalterable digital fingerprint that clarifies copyright ownership and provides solid copyright protection for creators. Ultimately, we aim to facilitate the exchange of globally popular cultures.
